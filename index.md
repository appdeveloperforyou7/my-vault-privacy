# Privacy Policy — My Vault

_Last updated: March 2026_

## Overview
My Vault is a local-first app. Your data stays on your device. We do not operate any servers, do not create user accounts, and do not collect personal information.

## Data stored on your device
The app stores the following data **locally and encrypted** on your device only:
- Passwords and login credentials
- Notes
- Tasks and habits
- Secure files

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly use the Export feature.

## Internet usage
The app uses the internet only for:
- **Feedback form** – if you choose to submit feedback, your message and optional email address are sent to the developer. This is entirely optional.
- **AI Agents** – if you configure an agent with your own Gemini API key, queries are sent directly to Google's Gemini API using your key. We do not see or store these requests.

## Biometric data
The app can optionally use your device's biometric authentication (fingerprint/face). Biometric data is handled entirely by your device's operating system and is never accessed or stored by the app.

## Third-party services
- **Google Gemini API** – only used if you provide your own API key. Subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
- **Google Play Billing** – used for the optional one-time unlock purchase. Subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## Children
This app is not directed at children under 13.
